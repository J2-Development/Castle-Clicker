Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026

Overview

Castle Clicker is a single-player idle game that runs entirely on your device. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data Collection

Castle Clicker does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. The game runs offline and all data stays on your device.

Local Storage

The game saves your progress using your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party. You can clear this data at any time through the in-game settings or your browser settings.

Third-Party Services

Castle Clicker does not use analytics, advertising, tracking pixels, or any third-party services that collect user data.

Fonts

The game loads fonts from Google Fonts on first use. Google's privacy policy applies to that request: policies.google.com/privacy.

Children's Privacy

Castle Clicker does not knowingly collect any information from children. The game does not require account creation or any personal information to play.

Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact
